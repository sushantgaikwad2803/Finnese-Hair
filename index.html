Our Story

Founded in 2010, Finnese Hair & Beauty Salon has been dedicated to providing exceptional beauty services in a luxurious and welcoming environment. Our founder, Isabella Finn, envisioned a space where clients could escape the stresses of daily life and indulge in self-care.

Over the years, we've built a reputation for excellence, combining traditional techniques with the latest trends in hair and beauty. Our team of certified professionals undergoes continuous training to stay at the forefront of the industry.

At Finnese, we believe that everyone deserves to feel beautiful and confident. We take the time to understand your unique needs and preferences, ensuring that you leave our salon looking and feeling your absolute best.
