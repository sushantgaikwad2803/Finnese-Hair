About Us

Learn more about our story and what makes Finesse special.

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Finesse Hair & Beauty Salon is dedicated to providing exceptional beauty services in a luxurious and welcoming environment.

Our founder, Rinku Sheth, envisioned a space where clients could escape the stresses of daily life and truly indulge in self-care. Over the years, we have built a reputation for excellence by combining traditional techniques with the latest trends in hair and beauty.

At Finesse, we believe that everyone deserves to feel beautiful and confident. Our team of certified professionals undergoes continuous training to stay at the forefront of the industry. We take the time to understand your unique needs and preferences, ensuring that you leave our salon looking and feeling your absolute best.

Owners
